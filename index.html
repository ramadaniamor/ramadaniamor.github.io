PR Days 2025 - Ahead of Time


Seminar "PR Days 2025: Ahead of Time" focused on the future of communication, exploring neuromarketing, social responsibility of influencers, branding in the age of experience economy, and the evolving relationship between media, sports, and politics. Throughout the program, seminar opened a dialogue on the directions in which PR, media, and society are heading.


Among the distinguished speakers were influencers Marta Lović, Veselin Vladimir Zvekić, Mara Ognjenović, AI and neuromarketing expert Nikola Učur, luxury branding strategist Ivana Laković, LoveFest marketing lead Adrijana Corelj, political analyst Prof. Dr. Miloš Bešić and director of the serbian news program of televison channel Nova S Slobodan Georgiev. Regional PR and communication experts Gordan Turković and Petar Tanta from CTA komunikacije also returned, delivering another dynamic session on the importance of creative courage and time in PR.


The 14th edition of PR Days was held on May 16 and 17, 2025, at the Faculty of Political Science, supported by "Zdravo dvadesete" platform, with contributions from over 15 partner brands and organizations.

PR Days 2024 - Start the Story


Seminar "PR Days 2024: Start the Story" dealt with communication strategy in the modern digital environment, exploring various aspects of marketing campaigns, the use of social networks, internal and external communication, as well as the application of artificial intelligence in marketing strategies.


Seminar brought together some of the well-known names of the domestic and Adria regional PR and marketing scene: consultant and founder of the TopIC platform Tijana Bejatović, communications director of the Nordeus Foundation Marija Beslać, PR expert Marko Nikolovski and PR manager of the Partizan Basketball Club and the Serbian men's A basketball team Ivan Ivković, marketing educator Vera Stevanović and AI educator Miloš Stevanović from Ludus Academy, Account Director Nikola Dragić and Senior Copywriter Vanja Bahilj from the advertising agency Ovation BBDO, Petar Tanta and Gordan Turković from agency CTA Communications from Zagreb, Assoc. Prof. Dr. Nikola Jović from the Faculty of Political Science and political marketing expert Aleksandar Belevski from North Macedonia.


PR Days 2024 was held on May 16 and 17, 2024 at the Faculty of Political Science.
